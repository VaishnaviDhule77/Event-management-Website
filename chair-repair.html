Frequently Asked Questions

Do you repair all types of chairs?

Yes, we repair all types of chairs including office chairs, dining chairs, armchairs, recliners, and antique chairs. Our team has expertise in various mechanisms and materials.

Can you restore antique chairs?

Absolutely! We specialize in antique chair restoration, preserving the original character and value while ensuring structural integrity and functionality.

Do you provide upholstery services?

Yes, we offer professional upholstery services with a wide range of quality fabrics and materials. Our craftsmen can match existing upholstery or create entirely new designs.

Do you offer emergency chair repair services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency chair repair services with a response time of under 2 hours for urgent issues. Our team is always ready to address your chair emergencies.
