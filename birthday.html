Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a birthday party?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for larger parties and 2-3 weeks for smaller gatherings. This allows us to secure your preferred date and ensure all vendors are available.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we work with caterers who can accommodate various dietary restrictions including allergies, vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Just let us know in advance.

Do you provide decorations for outdoor parties?

Absolutely! We have experience with both indoor and outdoor party setups. Weather permitting, outdoor parties can be magical with tents, bouncy castles, and garden games.

Can parents stay during the party?

Yes, parents are always welcome to stay. We ensure there's a comfortable area for adults to relax while our team entertains the children.

Do you offer entertainment for different age groups?

Yes, we tailor entertainment to the age group. For younger children, we have gentle games and activities. For teens, we offer more sophisticated entertainment like DJs and photo booths.
